FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can Ascentry respond to a new enquiry?

We aim to respond to all enquiries within one business day. For urgent matters, you can call our office directly during business hours.

Do you offer a free IT assessment?

Yes — all new engagements start with a complimentary IT environment assessment to understand your current state and identify opportunities.

What geographies does Ascentry cover?

Ascentry is headquartered in Hyderabad with clients across pan-India. We also support international engagements for select managed service clients.

Can Ascentry take over an existing IT environment?

Absolutely. We have a structured onboarding process for transition engagements, ensuring continuity of service throughout the handover period.

Do you work with SMBs or only enterprise clients?

Ascentry works with organisations of all sizes — from 50-person companies to large enterprises. Our service tiers are designed to fit different scales and budgets.

What are your standard SLA terms?

Our standard managed services SLA includes 99.9% uptime, 4-hour on-site response, and 24/7 remote monitoring. Custom SLAs are available for enterprise engagements.
